Ezra also has an older brother Micah and little sister Olivia. His family has a rich artistic atmosphere, but his parents still were hesitant if Ezra would be a actor. Bird By The Pond Director: Uberto Pasolini Main actors and actresses: Ezra Pierce/Margot Robbie/Clémence Poésy /Hugh Grant It’s a movie about a normal poet, who didn’t have much talent. He can barely live in US, and asked money from his little brother, and then he flew to Paris. The poet’s name is Felix Smith. In Paris, Felix wandered in the streets and parks, he wrote poems for the tourist for the living. Most of the time, he is a homeless person. As he sleeped in the bench of Bois de Boulogne, a lady asked if he need any help. The lady is Blake Accola(Margot Robbie), she’s from Australia, and now she lived here with her husband. Blake is kind, and they became friends. Blake has friends in press, and she promised she could try to let his poems publish. Hope is a dangerous thing for Felix to have. Blake is not a therapist, but she can sensed that Felix is suffered from great depression. She wants to help, but it’s useless. The more she offers, the more Felix despises himself. After a long, terrible winter of waiting, and desperate love to Blake. Felix killed himself in the winter, he was drown in the lake, in the park he loves the most. The plot is simple. What the movie touches people most is the atmosphere of loneliness, of Who I Am. The long distance from ideal and reality hurts, and most of the people don’t have the talent they want.
